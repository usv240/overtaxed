About Event
​Website: https://triggerdev.clickhouse.com/
One week. One theme. Build something real.

​We're running our first ever virtual hackathon — co-hosted by ClickHouse and Trigger.dev, open to professional developers across NAMER and EMEA. No fluff, no filler. Just a week to ship a production-quality AI agent chat experience using two of the best developer tools in the stack.

​The theme

​Build a chat agent that changes how people interact with information. Trigger.dev handles the orchestration and background jobs. ClickHouse powers the real-time data layer underneath. Both are required — that's the point. You'll get free cloud credits for both platforms the moment you're accepted.

​What you're building for

​A jury of 10–15 judges will score all submissions across six criteria: use of ClickHouse & Trigger.dev (25%), problem fit (20%), technical implementation (20%), innovation (20%), scalability & impact (10%), and presentation (5%).

​Prizes — EUR 10,000 total pool

​🥇 Grand prize — Ultimate desk setup bundle (iMac, iPad Air, AirPod Max, Apple Watch accessories) + 2K ClickHouse Cloud credits + Trigger.dev credits

​🥈 Runner-up — Dev bundle (MacBook Air, AirPods Pro, accessories) + 1K ClickHouse Cloud credits + Trigger.dev credits

​⭐ Bonus category (best OLTP + OLAP integration) — Lego set of your choice + 500 ClickHouse Cloud credits + Trigger.dev credits

​🎉 Social raffle — A co-branded ClickHouse × Trigger.dev swag box, open to all registered participants

​The timeline

​29 June — Registrations open

​16 July — Applications close Midnight PST

​17 July, 09:00 CET — Problem statement revealed, build window opens

​23 July, Midnight AoE

​29 July — Winners announced

​Who can apply

​Professional developers aged 18+, based in the US, Canada, UK, or an EU country. Solo or teams of up to 5. This is an application-based event — we're looking for builders who have shipped real things and can work independently with minimal hand-holding. ClickHouse and Trigger.dev employees are not eligible.
Teams need to submit together under one captain!

​The rules in brief

​Your submission needs a public GitHub repo or in a cloud enviorment, a demo video on YouTube (max 5 minutes), and meaningful use of both ClickHouse and Trigger.dev. All code must be written during the build window. AI coding assistants, Copilot, Cursor, Claude, ChatGPT — are explicitly permitted and encouraged.

FAQ
Who can apply?
Professional developers, 18+, residing in the US, Canada, UK, or an EU country. Solo or teams up to 5. This is an application-based event — we're looking for awesome builders
Do I need to use both tools?
Yes. ClickHouse must be your primary database, and your solution must use Trigger.dev's chat.agent() as the orchestration layer. Submissions that don't meaningfully use both will be disqualified. You'll receive free cloud credits for both upon acceptance.
What do I submit?
A demo video (YouTube max 5 minutes — skip the intro, open directly with a screen recording of your working product) plus a public GitHub repo under an MIT or Apache 2.0 license. Submitted via the official submission portal, shared on July 17.
How does the application work?
Short application via Luma. We ask about your background, prior hackathon experience, and whether you've built AI agents before. Registration closes July 16 — accepted teams are notified by email.
When should I set up my accounts?
As soon as you're accepted — before July 17. Cloud credit provisioning can take up to 48 hours, so don't wait until the build window opens.
Is there support during the event?
Yes — a panel of 15+ judges and async support channels staffed by ClickHouse and Trigger.dev engineers throughout the week. AI coding assistants like Copilot, Cursor, Claude, and ChatGPT are explicitly permitted and encouraged.

## What is Trigger.dev?

Trigger.dev is an open source background jobs framework that lets you write reliable workflows in plain async code. Run long-running AI tasks, handle complex background jobs, and build AI agents with built-in queuing, automatic retries, and real-time monitoring. No timeouts, elastic scaling, and zero infrastructure management required.

We provide everything you need to build and manage background tasks: a CLI and SDK for writing tasks in your existing codebase, support for both [regular](/tasks/overview) and [scheduled](/tasks/scheduled) tasks, full observability through our dashboard, and a [Realtime API](/realtime) with [React hooks](/realtime/react-hooks#realtime-hooks) for showing task status in your frontend. You can use [Trigger.dev Cloud](https://cloud.trigger.dev) or [self-host](/self-hosting/overview) on your own infrastructure.
